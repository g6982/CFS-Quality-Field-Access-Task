Grant Fund Projects with Budget Management Enterprise

Analytic Budget Management for Grant / Fund Projects Enterprise

This app allows you to have management of your grants / fund projects with budget as grant seekers as well as grant makers.

Main Features:

  • Allow your grant / fund project team to create a project costing sheet for material, labour and overhead related to the projects of grant / fund seeking applications.
  • You can create a budget with selected *Grant Applications* for grant seeking applications.
  • You can create a budget with selected *Grant Types* for grant maker applications.
  • You can get more details about job costing sheet and material requisition in dependant apps by clicking links.https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/odoo_job_costing_management/ and https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/material_purchase_requisitions/.
  • You can also get more details about the Grant/Fund depend app here https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/grant_fund_manage_odoo/.
  • For budget, you can check dependant app https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/15.0/job_costing_budget_contracting_enterprice/.
  • For more details you can watch the video and check below screenshots.
